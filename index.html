About Me

Driven by a passion for coding and problem-solving, I began my journey in tech during high school, developing games in C++ and Java and delving into data exploration with SQL. My career took a pivotal turn when I entered the healthcare field, where I discovered the profound potential of technology to enhance patient care and address systemic disparities.

Today, I am committed to leveraging data, analytics, and innovative software solutions to streamline processes, increase efficiency, and uncover insights that drive impactful change. I believe in using technology to make healthcare more equitable and accessible, and I thrive on crafting solutions that bridge the gap between human intuition and data-driven discovery.

With each project, my goal is to deliver high-quality, user-centered solutions that not only improve workflows but also contribute meaningfully to the well-being of those served.
